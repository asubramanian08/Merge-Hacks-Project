About Us

Our Goal

One third of all the food produced is wasted! Yet over 820 million people are starving across the globe. Our goal for Save the Food is to help change this unfortunate situation by donating all the foods made by users to the homeless.

Save the Food offers a few ways for people to keep better track of the food that they have in order to minimize wastage. Given each individual's menu, they can purchase as much food as they want and if there are any items that can't be used, Save the Food will donate those leftovers to those in need.

What you can do

A few ways to prevent food wastage are

  • Make efficient trips to the store so you can save time and also gas
  • Save leftovers
  • Save seeds for fruits and vegetables, so that they can be regrown at home. (This is also cost-effective!)
