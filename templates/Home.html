Welcome to Save the Food

Here at Save The Food, we strive to help our users... save the food! First-time users can sign up to create an account for our website, and returning users will be able to log in. After signing up/logging in, there will be a list of recipes available to buy and make, and selecting a recipe also allows the user to choose the quantity of how many of each food to buy. Once they make their purchase, users see their "inventory" of foods. Users can select the done button to complete their cooking, and a prompt asks them if they have any leftovers. Selecting "OK" will donate all leftovers to homeless shelters.
